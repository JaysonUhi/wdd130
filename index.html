I hope to be some sort of developer in the future and hope my mechanical background will help me in troubleshooting problems in code. I am hoping that by going back to school and earning a degree, I will be able to have a longer and more rewarding career in the tech field. If I can find a job as a developer, it will allow me to hopefully last longer physically, as being a mechanic can sometimes be a pretty harsh job. I am also hoping that I will be able to spend more time with my family and love what I do and who I work for. Thank you for reading this if you have gotten this far.
